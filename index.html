Remarks:

  • Remark 1: India has 28 states and 8 union territories but the states.csv list 9 union territories by repeating "Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu" and "Daman and Diu" separately
  • Remark 2: Excel file has 739 districts, Json file has 730 districts name match up with excel and 9 are missing. Additionally, data is missing for 13 of these districts. So, we have supported 717 districts. Boundary or data for 22 districts need to be fixed.
  • Remark 3: Additionally, json has 2 districts that do not exist in excel. So, there are 15 (13+2) areas where there is no visualization.
  • Remark 4: Population data from 2011 has been projected to 2020 by uniformly increasing all district population by 10% (to match the overall population increase in India).
